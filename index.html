Why not just take a Staycation?

First of all let me say that staycations can be a great way to spend a little time off, however I find being away from
my home more to my liking. You can argue by definition every time we go to work or school that we are traveling
so what is the big deal? While this is technically travel it is mechanical, souless, unless of course you pass by some
great scenery every day for example if you lived in Duluth. Travel is a great way to step away from the familiar,
meet new people and learn more about yourself. If you can find time to get away whether it be locally, across
the country, or across the world, then take the chance and make some memories.
